About Us

We Leave A Delicious Memory For You

At Everyday Kitchen, we offer authentic Nigerian dishes in a relaxed, serene and cool settings in the city of Ibadan. Our dishes are spicy, tasteful and unequivocally African and we are very proud to serve them all with all the love in us..

Our Menu reflects the high diversity of dishes from Nigeria. Our focus is on well-sourced and freshly cooked ingredients. At Everyday Kitchen, you will be welcomed and served by friendly and efficient staff, in an atmosphere that is comfortable and relaxed. A mixture of modern and traditional ambiance, West African crafts displayed around which gives us a traditional feel.
